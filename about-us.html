Who we are

Nubiana Cocoa is chocolate company that makes its chocolate from authentic Ghanaian Cocoa.

These Chocolate bars are defined by creativity and authenticity. Nubiana Chocolate is committed to producing premium confectionaries to its consumers.

Produced and packaged in Ghana, the world’s second largest exporter of cocoa in the world. Nubiana uses the finest of Ghanaian cocoa to produce its premium chocolate infused with natural flavors and inclusions and is dedicated to creating stunning and delicious treats.

We partner with some of Ghana’s best manufactures and producers of high-quality cocoa beans, cocoa liqueur, cocoa butter and cocoa powder. Our chocolates are carefully crafted with the aim of giving our customers tasty, attractive, affordable chocolates with a touch of African history and heritage.

Our Story

Nubiana is a premium Ghanaian chocolate designed to suit the sophisticated taste buds of the African. This Chocolate has a unique feature, which is to narrates the African heritage to the world.

Team Nubiana identified a need of the African and the world wanting to learn and know more about the African history and heritage. This chocolate bar was created to imbibe the spirt of pride and Africanism into the African race and the rest of the world by Africans.

The Idea of Nubiana originated in 2020 at the peak of the pandemic where most people were home and had the quest to learn more, but it came into existence in 2021.The Cocoa used in the production of Nubiana Chocolate is mainly from the forest regions of Ghana. The second largest producer of cocoa in the world and a nation of rich cultural heritage.
